About Rio Dental Clinic

Trusted Since 2008

Your Trusted Family Dentist in San Jose

For over 15 years, Rio Dental Clinic has been providing exceptional dental care to families and professionals throughout San Jose and the greater Bay Area. Our practice was founded on the principle that everyone deserves access to high-quality, compassionate dental care in a comfortable and welcoming environment.

Our team of experienced dentists, hygienists, and support staff are dedicated to staying at the forefront of dental technology and techniques. We invest in ongoing education and state-of-the-art equipment to ensure our patients receive the best possible care.

At Rio Dental, we understand that visiting the dentist can be anxiety-inducing for some. That's why we've created a calming atmosphere and take the time to listen to your concerns, explain procedures thoroughly, and ensure you feel completely at ease throughout your visit.

Comprehensive Dental Services

We offer a full spectrum of dental treatments to meet the needs of every member of your family, from preventive care to advanced restorative procedures.

Preventive Dentistry

Regular cleanings, exams, and oral cancer screenings to maintain optimal dental health and catch issues early.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers to enhance your appearance and boost your confidence.

Restorative Dentistry

Fillings, crowns, bridges, and implants to restore function and aesthetics to damaged or missing teeth.

Orthodontic Treatments

Traditional braces and clear aligners to straighten teeth and correct bite issues for a healthier smile.

Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle, friendly dental care for children, creating positive early experiences for lifelong oral health.

Emergency Dentistry

Prompt, compassionate care for dental emergencies including toothaches, broken teeth, and trauma.
